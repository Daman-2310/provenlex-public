Architecture

Byzantine Fault Tolerant Consensus

No single bot can fire an alert alone. 7 of 11 bots must agree — tolerating up to 3 compromised or malfunctioning nodes.

Resilience

Three-Tier Self-Healing

Bots restart themselves. Feeds switch automatically. Humans are only paged when the situation genuinely requires it.

Tier 1 — AUTO
Silent Recovery
Bot restart, backup feed switch, bus reconnection — all automatic, no human required, resolved in under 5 seconds.
Tier 2 — NOTIFY
Commander Notified
Model drift, signature failure, consensus degradation — auto-resolved but Commander Bot logs and reports to operator.
Tier 3 — ESCALATE
Human Required
Byzantine node detected, unverified sanctions hit, data breach — human operator paged immediately. Permanent audit trail preserved.
